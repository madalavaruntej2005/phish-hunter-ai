# Privacy Policy - Phish Hunter AI Backend

## No Data Storage
- **No user data is stored on our servers.**
- All analysis is processed **in memory only** and **discarded immediately** after response.
- No logs contain user-submitted text.
- No PII (emails, phone numbers, etc.) retained.

## Rate Limiting
- Limited to 10 requests per minute per IP to prevent abuse.
- Contact for higher limits.

## Deployment
- Hosted on Render.com with HTTPS enforced.
- Automatic data discard policy.
